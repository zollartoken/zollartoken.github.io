Tokenomics Explanation

Burned

After each transaction, a percentage of that transaction will be burned, which means that the total token supply decreases and the tokens become more scarce.

Liquidity

In order to ensure that $Zollar can be exchanged on Pancakeswap, each transaction will have a percentage that directly flows back to the liquidity on Pancakeswap.

Marketing wallet

Zollar will fuel Z army with the full marketing power. With every single transaction, a fraction of token will be redisctibuted and we will make sure to bring more people to Z army.
